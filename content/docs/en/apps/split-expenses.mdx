Split Expenses tracks shared spending within a group. Create expense groups (home, trip, dinner club), add members, and record who paid what. Supports equal and custom splits, real-time balances, and optimal debt settlement.

## Features

- **Expense Groups**: Create groups with members, currency, and emoji
- **Equal & Custom Splits**: Split bills equally or set custom amounts per person
- **Transfers**: Record direct payments between members
- **Real-time Balances**: See who owes whom at a glance
- **Settlements**: Algorithm suggests minimum transfers to clear all debts
- **Tags**: Categorize expenses for better stats
- **Stats**: Spending breakdown by person, category, and time period
- **Mobile App**: Full native mobile experience with push notifications
- **Notifications**: Push + email notifications for new expenses and settlements
