## Overview

The 027Apps mobile app supports both light and dark mode. The app automatically follows your device's system preference.

## How It Works

1. The app reads your device's color scheme setting (light/dark)
2. All Design System components adapt automatically
3. The header and tab bar colors adjust accordingly

## Changing the Setting

**iOS**: Settings → Display & Brightness → Dark

**Android**: Settings → Display → Dark theme

The app updates immediately — no restart needed.

## Web App

The web app also supports dark mode. Toggle it from the profile menu in the top-right corner. Your preference is saved to your profile.
