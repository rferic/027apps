027Apps is an **open-source platform for group apps** — for families, teams, or any collective. Each group manages its own users, roles, and app permissions.

The platform exposes a **REST API** under `/api/v1/` for authentication, user management, app access, and locale configuration. An interactive OpenAPI reference is available at [/api-docs](/api-docs).

---
